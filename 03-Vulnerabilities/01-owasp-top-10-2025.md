## Bug Bounty Signal by Category

- Broken Access Control: ~30% of all valid findings (IDOR most common)
- Injection: ~15% (SQLi dropping, SSTI/NoSQL rising)
- Cryptographic Failures: ~8% (JWT issues, weak TLS, hardcoded keys)
- Insecure Design: ~12% (business logic, rate limiting gaps)
- SSRF: ~5% but highest average payout (cloud metadata)

## Data Categories
- A01:2025 – Broken Access Control: Most prevalent in bug bounty
- A03:2025 – Injection: NoSQL and SSTI rising, SQLi declining
- A10:2025 – SSRF: Highest average payout due to cloud impact
- A04:2025 – Insecure Design: Business logic flaws escape automation
