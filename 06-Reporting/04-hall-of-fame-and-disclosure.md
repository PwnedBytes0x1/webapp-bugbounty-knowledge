# Hall of Fame & Disclosure: 2026 Reference

## Hall of Fame
- Many programs include HoF recognition even without bounty
- Criteria varies: valid critical finding = HoF, or N valid reports
- Some exclude automated scanner output from HoF consideration

## Disclosure Options
1. **Full disclosure**: Complete details + PoC, all parties at once
2. **Responsible disclosure**: Vendor gets fix window, then public
3. **Coordinated disclosure**: Vendor + reporter publish simultaneously
4. **Partial disclosure**: Bug class writeup, no vendor identifying info

## Timeline Norms
- Initial response: 1-5 business days
- Triage + validation: 1-2 weeks
- Fix deployment: 30-90 days (varies by severity)
- Bounty payment: 14-30 days after triage acceptance
- Public disclosure: 90-180 days after initial report

## Legal Protection
- Safe harbor: Platform policy protects researchers acting in good faith
- Authorized scope: Only test defined targets
- No extortion: Never threaten negative disclosure for bounty
- Data handling: Delete any accessed user data after reproduction
