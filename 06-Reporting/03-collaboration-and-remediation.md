# Collaboration & Remediation: 2026 Guide

## During Triage
- Program may ask clarifying questions — respond same-day
- Provide additional proof if requested (curl commands, video)
- Accept reasonable fixes and verify before closing
- Don't demand bounty amount — negotiate only if promised

## Responsible Disclosure
- Follow program disclosure timeline (usually 90 days)
- For no-fix: escalate to CERT/CSIRT after timeline
- Public disclosure: coordinate with vendor
- Partial disclosure: class-of-bug writeup without identifying info

## Remediation Verification
- Confirm fix in 2-3 weeks after patch
- Test regression: confirm same bug class doesn't recur
- Check for incomplete fixes (filtered but not fixed)
- Verify in production (not just staging)

## Bounty Collection
- Platforms: HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Intigriti, Synack
- Payment methods: PayPal, Bank transfer, Crypto (varies)
- Tax implications: Self-reported in most jurisdictions
- Average resolution time: 14-30 days for valid reports
