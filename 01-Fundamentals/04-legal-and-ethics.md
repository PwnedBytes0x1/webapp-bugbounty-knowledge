# Legal & Ethics

## Key Legal Concepts
- **Authorization Is Everything** - Scope is a limited license to test
- **Data Protection Laws** - GDPR (EU), CCPA (CA), PIPEDA (Canada)
- **Disclosure Laws** - Most programs require approval before disclosure

## Ethical Guidelines
### Do's
- Stay strictly within defined scope
- Stop testing immediately if you encounter PII
- Use a dedicated testing email/account
- Encrypt communications with the program

### Don'ts
- Don't use automated scanners that could cause DoS
- Don't modify or delete data
- Don't publicly disclose without approval
- Don't demand payment or threaten disclosure

## International Considerations
| Country | Key Law | Risk Level |
|---------|---------|------------|
| USA | CFAA, DMCA | High |
| UK | Computer Misuse Act | Medium |
| EU | GDPR + national laws | Medium |
| UAE/Saudi | Cybercrime laws | VERY HIGH |
| China | Cybersecurity Law | VERY HIGH |

Recommendation: Only test on platforms that provide safe harbor.
